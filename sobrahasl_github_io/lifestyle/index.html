# FLEXIBILITY : A lifestyle without flexibility will be cracked with very simple changes. Flexibility to me is a key to arrange and adapt my life style to environment and other variables.

# PRAY AND RELAX : All creatures need a good relationship with their creator. Praying not only help me to be relax but also help me to understand who I am really.

# PROGRAMMING : There are lots of situations that I need a loyal friend who do my repetitive tasks. Computers to me is the most reliable friend that never says " no " to me . You just need learn how talk to it.

# LEARNING : Reading books is a great source to find answers for my unsolved questions. Especially philosophy books which are a combination of logic and sciences which cannot be felt by senses.

# NATURE : Nature is our real birthplace. Coming back to nature sometimes can help me to see the real beauty which is invisible in mega cities. And i shouldn't forget the Fresh air.

# DOCUMENTARIES : Instead of watching fiction movies , I prefer to watch documentaries that are based on reality. Believe it , reality is more mysterious than fiction movies

# DESIGN : For leisure time , I really like to design , edit and create photos , videos and motion graphics. Just for fun.
